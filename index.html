Teak Polymer Composites

Teak Polymer composites specialize in the manufacturing of Wood Plastic Composites (WPC). We do so by creating a mixture of recycled wood dust from the furniture industry, or nowadays also with Rice Husk as a side stream from agriculture. Together with some performance additives we create a compound that is extremely versatile and can be extruded into several WPC profiles.

Our products range from simple decking to more complex applications such as components for doors and fire-retardant wall cladding. Our customers vary from distributors and contractors to other manufacturers that use our composites in their final products. We are always searching to expand our horizon to find new applications whether it is domestic or world-wide. If we can be of service to you, do not hesitate to get in touch.
